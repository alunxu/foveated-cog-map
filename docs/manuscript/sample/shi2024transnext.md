# TransNeXt: Robust Foveal Visual Perception for Vision Transformers

- **Authors**: Dai Shi
- **Venue**: Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR) 2024
- **Year**: 2024
- **CVF**: https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/CVPR2024/papers/Shi_TransNeXt_Robust_Foveal_Visual_Perception_for_Vision_Transformers_CVPR_2024_paper.pdf

## Abstract (paraphrased)
TransNeXt introduces a "Aggregated Pixel-focused Attention" mechanism inspired by biological foveal vision and continuous eye movement, modelling fine-grained focused perception in the centre with broader peripheral perception. It produces strong results on ImageNet, COCO and ADE20K backbones at modest cost, and is one of the cleanest demonstrations that foveal-prior attention is competitive with global ViT attention.
